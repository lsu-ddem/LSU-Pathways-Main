## For Teachers
<br>
In order to teach an LSU Pathway course or module, no prior STEM experience is assumed. A teacher simply needs STEM-curiosity, enthusiasm for project-based teaching, and willingness to attend a virtual summer training workshop. Throughout the school year, teachers receive continual assistance from, and access to, LSU STEM Pathwaysâ€™ support staff.
